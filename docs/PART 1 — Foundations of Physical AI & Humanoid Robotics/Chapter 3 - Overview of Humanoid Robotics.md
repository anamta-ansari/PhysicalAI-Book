# Chapter 3: Overview of Humanoid Robotics

## Types of robots (industrial, service, humanoid, quadruped)
- **Industrial robots**: Automation in manufacturing
- **Service robots**: Assistance in homes, hospitals, and workplaces
- **Humanoids**: Human-like robots capable of versatile tasks
- **Quadrupeds**: Four-legged robots designed for rough terrain

## Human-robot interaction basics
Principles for safe and intuitive communication using gestures, speech, and sensors.

## Sensors used in humanoid robots
- LiDAR
- Cameras
- IMUs
- Tactile sensors
- Force sensors

## Applications: Education, Healthcare, Defense, Household automation, Manufacturing
Humanoids assist in teaching, medical care, security, domestic chores, and industrial processes.
